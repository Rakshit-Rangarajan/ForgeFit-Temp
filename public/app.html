What's inside

EVERY TOOL
YOU NEED.

Built for someone starting from zero — no gym, no experience, no idea where to start. ForgeFit handles everything.

🔐
Private Accounts
No public signup. Accounts created directly via SQL by the admin. JWT auth, 30-day sessions.
🤖
AI Coach
Claude AI via Puter.js — completely free. Knows your BMI, goal, fitness level, and Indian dietary context.
📊
Health Report AI
Upload your MovingLife / HealthSense screenshot. AI reads it, extracts metrics, builds a personalised plan.
📈
Progress Tracking
PostgreSQL-backed weight, BMI, and session logs with Chart.js graphs. Synced across all devices.
🏋️
Goal-Aware Workouts
Exercise plans adapt to your goal — fat loss, abs, muscle, recomposition. No equipment needed.
🔔
Smart Alarms
Water, meal, workout, and sleep reminders. Audio alarm, browser notification, haptic vibration, snooze.
📱
Samsung Health
Export JSON from Samsung Health and import weight + step data directly. No third-party sync needed.
⌚
Wear OS Layout
Minimal watch UI at ?watch=1. Streak, water, steps, next alarm. Tap to log workout or water.
